About Fate Project

Fate Project is a developing digital ecosystem based on the FATE token.

The project combines utility tools, symbolic digital products, decision-support concepts and future legacy-oriented features.

Fate Project is currently in an early development stage. The website, public documentation, official links and ecosystem structure are being prepared step by step.

Ecosystem Tools

Fate Project is planned as an ecosystem of digital tools and symbolic products connected by the FATE token.

  • FATE Calculator — a planned utility tool for calculating deals, profit, goals, margins, risks and decision scenarios.
  • FATE Flip — a planned coin-flip decision tool inspired by the symbolic idea of choice and personal destiny.
  • FATE Legacy — a planned direction focused on digital family memory, legacy symbols and future NFT/NFC integrations.

Additional tools may be added in the future only after testing and public readiness.
